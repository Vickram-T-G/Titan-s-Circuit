how to play:

1) players take turns placing titans on the outer circuit or moving existing ones.

2) when a circuit is fully filled, the inner circuit gets unlocked.

3) points are earned by controlling edges (having titans on both ends).

4) a titan surrounded by opponent titans(after placing all titans) is removed from play.

5) the player with the highest score wins when time runs out or the inner circuit is filled.
